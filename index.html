How to play
1. Earn resources each turn — 1 per networked hex of that type.
2. Trade: swap 2 of any resource for 1 of another using the Trade panel.
3. Drag tokens from trays onto a build slot.
4. Click Place → then click a yellow-highlighted hex.
5. Click End Turn — drought spreads.
6. Connect 70% of communities to win.
💧
Well
Provides clean water to a community. Connects one hex directly adjacent to your network.
Cost: 1 Funding1 Labor
Reach: 1 step from network.
🔧
Pipeline
Extends your network further — you choose exactly which hex to connect, letting you route around terrain you don't want.
Cost: 2 Materials
Reach: up to 2 steps. Fills in the path automatically.
🏭
Treatment Station
Most powerful building. Connects a hex AND purifies drought neighbors. Creates a barrier that slows drought spread.
Cost: 2 Funding1 Labor1 Materials
Reach: 1 step + drought shield.
☠️
Drought
Spreads from the top-left each turn. Destroys communities before you reach them. Networked hexes are immune. Treatment stations block spread to neighbors.
Lose if: drought claims 40% of communities.
Real impact: A single well serves ~300 people for 20 years. 100% of donations go directly to water projects.
